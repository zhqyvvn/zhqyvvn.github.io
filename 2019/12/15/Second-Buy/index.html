🌟 Components

• Database —> MongoDB
• Deployment —> Heroku

🌟 Features

Authentication:
• login / signup / logout

Index Page:
• Browse posts of second-hand books on sale published by other users
• Search sale information of specific second-hand books by the search bar
• Brief profile information on side

Profile Page:
• Edit username and contact information
• Check the number of own books on sale / the number of own books sold
• Browse brief information of own posts
• Other users can check the profile but only in guest view (editing functions are hidden)

Sell Book Page (also Edit Book information Page):
• Create a new post by filling the information form and upload the picture of the book
• Edit one existing post later
• Mark the book ‘on sale’ or ‘sold’

Book Detail Page:
• Show the owner of the book and other books information
• Other users can claim the book by clicking “I want this” to send a notification to the owner
• Comment and reply under the post

Notification Page:
Receive notifications if
• some users want to buy certain own books
• some users commented under certain own posts
• some users replied own comments under certain posts

📌 Note

• Information of books in the example were randomly selected from U of T library
• Background image (and alt image in the ‘upload the picture of the book’ area) was from Google Image
• Modified from my course project. Thanks to teammates Alex, Rita, Hlib and our instructor Mark
